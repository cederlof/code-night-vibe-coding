# Vision

A browser-based time trial maze game where you control a car with realistic momentum and drift.

## Core Appeal
- Master the balance between speed and control
- Beat your personal best time
- Tight, responsive physics that reward skill
- Immersive 3D third-person perspective

## Visual Presentation
- **3D View:** Third-person camera positioned above and behind the car
- **High Maze Walls:** Walls extend vertically to create depth and challenge
- **Spatial Awareness:** 3D perspective helps judge turns and distances

## Scope
Single static maze, keyboard controls, time tracking, 3D rendering via lightweight npm library. No audio, power-ups, or multiplayer in v1.
