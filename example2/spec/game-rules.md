# Game Rules

## Objective
Navigate the car from start to the highlighted goal as fast as possible.

## Physics
- **Acceleration:** Hold UP to accelerate to max speed
- **Braking:** Press DOWN to slow down
- **Friction:** Car decelerates when no input is pressed
- **Steering:** LEFT/RIGHT rotate the car with momentum/drift
- **Wall collision:** Car rebounds and loses speed temporarily

## Timing
- Timer starts on first movement input
- Current time displayed during play
- Best time saved in browser storage

## Restart
Press R (or on-screen button) to restart from beginning.
