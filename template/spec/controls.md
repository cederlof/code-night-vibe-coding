# Controls

## Gameplay
- **Start Button**: Initialize race (players must press movement keys to begin moving)
- **Player 1 Jump**: W key to make runner jump over obstacles
- **Player 1 Move**: D key to move forward (press repeatedly for speed)
- **Player 2 Jump**: Numpad 8 to make runner jump over obstacles
- **Player 2 Move**: Numpad 6 to move forward (press repeatedly for speed)
- **Clear Best Button**: Reset personal best time

## Movement Mechanics
- Players control speed by pressing movement key (D for P1, Numpad 6 for P2)
- Faster key presses = faster movement speed
- Slower key presses or no presses = speed decreases
- Speed has minimum and maximum limits

## Timing
- Jump must be timed to clear obstacles
- No horizontal movement control
- No ducking or sliding
